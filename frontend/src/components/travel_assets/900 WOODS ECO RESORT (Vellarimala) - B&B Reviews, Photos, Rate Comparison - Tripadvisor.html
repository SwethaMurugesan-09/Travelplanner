Great getaway in the wild
We spent 2 nights at 900woods as a group of 6 and it was very lovely and I wish we would have stayed longer. :)

The food was great, not spicy at all when we asked if this was possible.

The housing was simplistic but with everything one requires in the “jungle”. Warm water in the morning and evening and a comfortable bed.

I really enjoyed getting away from the hustle of the city, to reconnect with nature- being woken up by birds in the morning and seeing the red squirrel were a few highlights.

The staff are very friendly and eager to help out with questions and organising activities.

Since the location is quite remote, we had to learn to be patient since not everything works according to schedule- like waiting for a jeep longer than expected, but this also adds to the charm of the place.

If you are looking for a place to unwind and enjoy the great outdoors, I’d definitely recommend staying at 900woods. :)

Ow. Wow
Fantastic place. Loved this resort.

We stayed here with family for one night. The facilities and the experience is awesome. We took three rooms out of their four room house. The house itself is very eco friendly and in a traditional and natural style. The resort is loke inside the forest (it is fully lit throughout night) with no other buildings or people nearby. Very serene and peaceful setting.

The staff are extremely friendly and helpful. They take care of all of your needs very pleasantly. They arrange for any extra stuff you need, hot water, night campfire, morning trekking etc. Our day is fully planned by them for us. Lovely.

Need a separate para for the food they provide. It was just atipoli - super yummy delicious. Great variety, good quantity, and served with love. The cook is a great person and knows his job very well.

Overall, the stay was fantaboulus. And not that costly.

Don't miss the glass bridge and the jeep ride to this place. It is better than a roller-coaster experience.
